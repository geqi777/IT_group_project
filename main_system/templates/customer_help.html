Frequently Asked Questions

You can book an e-vehicle by navigating to the booking section on your dashboard, selecting the vehicle of your choice, and choosing the time slot that suits you.
To cancel a booking, go to "My Bookings" under your dashboard, click on the booking you wish to cancel, and select the "Cancel Booking" option.
If you face any issue with your vehicle, please report it immediately through the "Report Issue" section in your dashboard or contact customer support for further assistance.
